## Implementation Notes

### Task Selection Criteria
1. **Impact on repository quality**: How much does this improve instruction effectiveness?
2. **Effort to value ratio**: Is the expected effort justified by the outcome?
3. **Dependency chain**: What other tasks does this enable?
4. **User benefit**: How does this help people writing better AI instructions?

### Success Metrics
- **Knowledge Integration**: Number of research insights successfully documented
- **Quality Improvement**: Reduction in ambiguous or low-quality instructions
- **Usability Enhancement**: Improved navigation and discoverability
- **Community Value**: Adoption and feedback from instruction usage

### Risk Management
- **Scope creep**: Keep tasks focused and well-defined
- **Quality degradation**: Maintain review standards during rapid development
- **Tool dependency**: Ensure instructions remain platform-agnostic where possible
- **Maintenance burden**: Avoid creating unsustainable complexity
